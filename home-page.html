Repeat Click has been developed to provide the long-term sustainability of your local community pharmacy without having to compromise on convenience with the repeat click APP. The APP allows you to order your medication without leaving your home, with the security of knowing that the medication you receive is dispensed by your local, trained and trusted pharmacist.

Living in a digital era pharmacists recognise that our current culture and expectations have become accustomed to convenience alongside immediacy with the use of modern services such as Amazon, Uber and Netflix. Repeat Click provides unparalleled access to a trusted healthcare setting immediately and conveniently without compromising on your values of supporting local businesses and the local economy.
