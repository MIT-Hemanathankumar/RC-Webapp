About Us

STAY LOCAL STAY SAFE

As a working pharmacist and pharmacy owner I am passionate about providing quality care to my patients. I am privileged and proud to be at the heart of the local community alongside the local GP practice. As with many important local businesses and pharmacy branches I am becoming increasingly concerned about the sustainability of local community pharmacies in addition to the safety of my patients as a result of new online pharmacies. While online pharmacies provide much needed convenience for patients, there are significant hidden risks to patient safety. One of the biggest challenges is related to partnership working between the patient, GP and pharmacist. Seamless and expert communication between all stakeholders is essential for patient safety and accountability. The development of Repeat Click is a solution to maintain communication, accountability, multi-partnership working, expertise and safety without compromising on convenience for the patient. I know patients want to support and protect their local community pharmacy and its sustainability. Such loyalty should not mean having to compromise on convenience. Repeat Click enables you to protect your Local Community Pharmacy and its vital role in the local economy. Loyal, safe, local and trusted with the ease of Repeat Click.

Community Pharmacists deliver a wide range of services such as NHS Health Checks, smoking cessation, vaccinations, substance misuse services, sexual health screening, emergency contraception and many more. The Repeat Click AAP will keep you up to date regarding such services and appointments can be booked conveniently via the APP with teleconsultations available.
